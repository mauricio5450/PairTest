Features - Shaun Christie
	Pause- 
	The pause button will only be available during battle scenarios in the battle scene.
It will take a Keyboard input that will toggle the time delta of all objects in the scene to 0.

	Main Menu-
	The menu will be the start screen after execution of the application. The Main menu will have 
button panels that load the overworld. From the overworld you can exit back to the main menu. Then the
last button on the main menu will be to close the application. 

	Health Bar-
	The health bar will represent the total health of the player. It will change depending on the damage
value from enemy attacks. If the total health of the player is less than or equal to 0 after an enemy attack.
This will trigger the loss flag, loading you back to the overworld due to loosing the battle.
